FORESEEABLE Publisher

Privacy Policy

Effective 13 July 2026 Contact kyle.zammit@hotmail.co.uk

FORESEEABLE Publisher is a private, single-user tool operated by the owner of the FORESEEABLE channel (@watchforeseeable). It exists solely to upload and schedule the channel's own videos to the channel's own social accounts through official platform APIs, including the TikTok Content Posting API.

What we collect

The application collects and stores only the account authorization tokens for the operator's own accounts (issued through each platform's OAuth flow). It collects nothing else.

  • No data about any other user, viewer, or third party.
  • No analytics on people, no advertising identifiers, no cookies.
  • No website visitors — the app has no public interface and no other users.

How we use it

Authorization tokens are used exclusively to upload the channel's own video files and captions to the channel's own accounts. Data is never sold, rented, shared, or transferred to any third party, and is never used for any purpose other than publishing the operator's own content.

Storage & security

Tokens are stored locally on the operator's personal machine in a private, access-restricted configuration file. There is no cloud database and no third-party data processor. Tokens are transmitted only to the official platform API endpoints they authenticate against, over HTTPS.

Data retention & deletion

The operator can revoke the application's access at any time from the platform's own app settings, and can delete stored tokens by removing them from the local configuration file. Because no data about any other person is ever collected, there is no other user data to retain or delete. Requests can be sent to kyle.zammit@hotmail.co.uk.
